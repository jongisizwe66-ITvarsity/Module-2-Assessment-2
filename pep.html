PEP is a South African institution that started in 1965 with one store. Today PEP is the largest single brand retailer in the country with stores in almost every town and village, where it often provides a lifeline to local people with affordable, good quality merchandise. PEP’s essentials include clothing and footwear, cellular products and airtime, home ware, FMCG products as well financial products and services such as funeral cove, e-wallet, account payments Top T.V and loans. Over half of all PEP’s store managers are promoted from within the company. The company believes that its success lies in giving customers what they want. The PEP vision, mission and values verify their focus on exceptional customer service that results in delighted customers. The company values honesty, passion and resourcefulness. PEP’s flagship CSI project is the PEP Academy. There are now ten PEP academies within existing schools that help give thousands of Grade 4 learners vital education support in numeracy and literacy.
