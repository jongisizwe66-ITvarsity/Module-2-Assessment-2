Nu Metro is one of South Africa’s leading cinema chains, with a history going back to the first Metro Theatre in Johannesburg, 1932. Since 2014, Nu Metro has enhanced cinemagoing around South Africa through the introduction of cutting-edge cinema experiences 4DX, Xtreme and VIP at select cineplexes nationwide. Nu Metro strives to offer consumers an exceptional and unmatched cinema experience through regular talent visits by movie stars; competitions, promotions and activations; popular pre-release screenings and partnerships with some of South Africa’s leading brands; combined with the highest level of personalised service and the very best in sound and projection technology.
